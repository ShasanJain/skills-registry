# 📛 YouTube Titles: Viral Hook Engine

This skill engineers titles that command clicks by leveraging psychological triggers.

## 🛡️ 1. The Title Pillars
1.  **Negative Parallelism**: "Don't do X, do Y" or "Why your X is failing."
2.  **The Curiosity Gap**: Mention a result without the method.
3.  **Specific Numbers**: Use "7-Step" or "$1,240" instead of "many" or "money."
4.  **Reassurance**: "It's easier than you think" or "No experience required."

## 🏗️ 2. Industrial Title Protocol
- **Length**: Under 50 characters (mobile friendly).
- **Format**: [Subject] + [Dramatic Action] + [Benefit/Mystery].
- **Rule**: Never repeat the thumbnail text.
