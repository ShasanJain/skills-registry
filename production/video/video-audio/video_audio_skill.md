# 🔊 Video Audio: Sonic Architect

This skill engineers the auditory experience, including narration and atmospheric soundscapes.

## 🛡️ 1. The Audio Pillars
1.  **Voice Persona**: Define age, tone, and accent (e.g., "Deep, gravelly narrator," "Energetic 20s female").
2.  **Emotional Pacing**: Align speech rate with the visual energy.
3.  **Background Synergy**: Choose music that complements the scene without overpowering the voice.
4.  **SFX Integration**: Identify moments for foley (e.g., "Footsteps on gravel," "Wind howling").

## 🏗️ 2. Industrial Audio Protocol
- **Script**: The text to be spoken.
- **Provider**: ElevenLabs, OpenAI, or Cartesia.
- **Music Style**: Genre, BPM, and mood.

## ⌨️ Commands
| Command | Action |
| :--- | :--- |
| `/video-audio [text]` | Calls `video_audio.py` to generate TTS prompts and music styles. |
